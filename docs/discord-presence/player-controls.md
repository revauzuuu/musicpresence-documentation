# Player Controls

This guide covers how to control Discord Presence per media player.

## Toggling the presence per application

You can toggle a media player on or off from Music Presence's tray menu:

![Toggling from tray menu](/_static/media/toggle-presence-from-tray-menu-dark.png#only-dark)
![Toggling from tray menu](/_static/media/toggle-presence-from-tray-menu-light.png#only-light)

It is also possible to toggle media players in Music Presence's settings (under "Discord" in version 2.3.5 and older, or "Discord -> Settings" in version 2.3.6 and newer):

![Toggling from settings](/_static/media/toggle-presence-from-settings-dark.png#only-dark)
![Toggling from settings](/_static/media/toggle-presence-from-settings-light.png#only-light)

!!! info "Multimedia and radio players"
    Multimedia and radio players are disabled by default, since they could leak sensitive data or personal information. They must be enabled explicitly.
